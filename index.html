Projects

Concentration

A memory game where the player clicks on squares to reveal images. When two of the same image is chosen a match is made. The player wins when all the images are matched with its pair. Concentration was build with jQuery, HTML, and CSS.

Twain

Twain is a social media/trading website for readers built with Ruby on Rails and Bootstrap. Users can create a profile and upload books that they have and view other users books. Users can email other users to suggest trades. Users can also filter books by tag on the index page.

Rembr

Have you ever stood somewhere and wondered who else had been there? What had they thought as they stood there? Rembr gives you insight into who's been where you are. Users of Rembr can post and view perspectives based on a location. Rembr was a team project built with MongoDB, Express, Node.js, Bootstrap, and Mapbox.
